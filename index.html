Rooster Run Out Now

Escape the barn and compete against family and friends to win freedom but remember only one Rooster can escape, the other will be a sunday roast.

HOW TO PLAY

Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to play Rooster run.
UP to jump, RIGHT to go right, and LEFT to go left.

TUTORIAL

FEATURES

MUD

Mud will slow you down. Make sure you avoid it.

TNT

TNT will blow you up and take a life away.

LIGHTNING

Lightning will increase your speed. Make sure you collect it before your opponent does
